LINUX SERVER

ARCH LINUX | 3D PRINTING | FUSION360

This project began from an interest in working with X86 Linux systems to build my system administration skills. Due to hardware price increases from AI datacenter applications and other political pressure, building my own ATX system was off the table. I turned to my high school laptop, an Acer Travelmate P, to transform into a non-destructive (would be able to put back together as a laptop) headless 1U rack-mountable server.

This has also given me a reason to build my CAD skills designing a fully custom case for the motherboard and drive bays. Current challenges include getting the BIOS on the laptop motherboard, which is designed for school system administration and is relatively limited in its capabilities to play nicely with full 3.5" enterprise HDDs.
